About CareerHunt

CareerHunt is a modern job portal designed to simplify the hiring process for both job seekers and employers. We help passionate individuals connect with the right companies and grow their careers faster.

Founded in 2025, CareerHunt focuses on:

  • Promoting transparent, bias-free hiring
  • Empowering candidates across India to find the right job
  • Offering simple and clean UI for easy navigation

Whether you're an experienced professional or a fresher, CareerHunt is here to guide your journey and match your potential with top opportunities.
